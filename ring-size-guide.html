How to Measure Your Ring Size
🧵

Method 1: String

  1. Wrap a thin string around the base of your finger
  2. Mark where the string overlaps with a pen
  3. Lay the string flat on a ruler
  4. Measure the length in mm — this is your circumference
  5. Match to the size chart below
📏

Method 2: Existing Ring

  1. Find a ring that fits your finger well
  2. Place it flat on a ruler
  3. Measure the inner diameter in mm
  4. Match to the diameter column in the chart below
  5. Round up if you're between sizes

Tips for an Accurate Measurement
🕐
Measure at the end of the dayFingers are slightly larger in the evening due to temperature and activity. Measure then for a comfortable fit.
🌡️
Avoid cold fingersCold makes fingers shrink. Measure when your hands are warm for a true size.
↕️
Between two sizes? Go largerA ring that's slightly loose is more comfortable and easier to remove than one that's tight.
💍
Most Minella rings are adjustableMany of our rings are open-band or adjustable style — they fit most finger sizes without needing an exact measurement.
