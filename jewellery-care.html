✦ Care Guide

Jewellery Care

Our anti-tarnish jewellery is built to last. Follow these simple tips to keep your pieces looking brand new for longer.

💧

Waterproof

Safe in rain, sweat, and everyday wear. Avoid prolonged soaking in chlorinated or salt water.

✨

Anti-Tarnish Coating

A special protective layer prevents discolouration. Avoid abrasive cleaners that can strip it.

🧴

Avoid Chemicals

Put jewellery on after applying perfume, lotion, sunscreen, or hairspray — not before.

📦

Proper Storage

Store each piece separately in a dry, cool place. Use the pouch or box it came in.

🧽

Gentle Cleaning

Wipe with a soft, dry cloth after each use. For deeper cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth only.

🌙

Remove at Night

Take off your jewellery before sleeping and before swimming to extend its life significantly.

Do's & Don'ts
✓ Do's
  • Wear daily for regular use
  • Wipe with a soft cloth after wearing
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep pieces separate to avoid scratching
  • Put jewellery on last, after makeup & perfume
  • Handle gently, especially clasps
✗ Don'ts
  • Don't use harsh chemicals or bleach
  • Don't scrub with abrasive cloth
  • Don't wear in swimming pools or the sea
  • Don't store in humid bathrooms
  • Don't apply perfume directly on jewellery
  • Don't sleep with jewellery on
How to Clean Your Jewellery

Follow these simple steps to restore shine when your jewellery looks dull:

1
Remove and inspectTake off the jewellery and check for any loose parts or damage before cleaning.
2
Wipe with a dry soft clothUse a microfibre or jewellery polishing cloth. Gently buff in one direction — don't scrub in circles.
3
For stubborn dirtDampen the cloth very slightly with plain water. Wipe gently and let it air dry completely before storing.
4
Dry thoroughlyMake sure no moisture remains before storing. Trapped moisture speeds up tarnishing.
5
Store properlyPlace in the original pouch or a soft-lined box, away from other pieces that might scratch it.
Storage Tips

Proper storage is one of the biggest factors in how long your jewellery lasts. Here's what we recommend:

Store each piece individually — mixing them together causes scratches and tangling. Keep them in a dry, cool spot away from direct sunlight and humidity. A bedroom drawer or a jewellery organiser box is ideal. Avoid leaving jewellery on a bathroom shelf where it is exposed to steam and moisture daily.

If you're storing pieces for a long time, place a small silica gel packet in the box to absorb moisture and further prevent tarnishing.
