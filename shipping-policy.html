How We Ship

All orders are shipped from Coimbatore (641025) via trusted courier partners including Delhivery, India Post, and BlueDart. Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number on your registered mobile number via WhatsApp or SMS.

Order Processing

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2 PM or on Sundays are processed the next business day.
  • During sale periods, processing may take up to 3 business days.

Cash on Delivery

COD is available across India. A small COD handling charge (₹40 or 2% of order value, whichever is higher) is added at checkout.

🔄 Returns & Exchange Policy

7-day return window from the date of delivery for eligible items.

Eligible for Return

  • Item received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong item was delivered
  • Item is significantly different from the product description

Not Eligible for Return

  • Items that have been worn or used
  • Items without original packaging
  • Items purchased during clearance or final sale
  • Customised or personalised orders

How to Initiate a Return

WhatsApp us at our support number with your order ID, the reason for return, and clear photos of the item. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with the next steps.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we'll process your refund within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. COD orders are refunded via bank transfer.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, orders can be cancelled before they are dispatched. WhatsApp us immediately after placing the order if you wish to cancel.

What if my package is lost in transit?

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, contact us with your tracking number and we'll investigate and resolve it for you.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within India. International shipping is something we're working on!

Is my jewellery insured during shipping?

All orders are carefully packed to prevent damage. In the rare case of transit damage, please photograph the damaged package before opening and contact us immediately.
