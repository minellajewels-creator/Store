Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2025

We ship all orders across India with care. Each piece is securely packaged to ensure it reaches you safely.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are processed the next business day.

Delivery Timeline

ZoneEstimated Delivery
Metro cities (Chennai, Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.)3–5 business days
Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities5–7 business days
Remote / rural areas7–10 business days

Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier delays, weather, or local conditions beyond our control.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on your delivery pincode and order weight. The exact amount is shown before you confirm payment.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via SMS/email. You can also track your order anytime via our Order Tracking page.

Failed Delivery

If a delivery attempt fails due to an incorrect address or unavailability, the courier will attempt re-delivery. After 2–3 failed attempts, the package may be returned to us. Re-shipping charges will apply for re-dispatch.

Damaged in Transit

If your item arrives damaged, please refer to our Return Policy. Remember: an unboxing video is mandatory for any damage claim.
